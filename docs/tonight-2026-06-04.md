### Note on the postal address

The address `447 Broadway, 2nd Fl. — #3000, New York, NY 10013-2562`
used across `/privacy`, `/terms`, `/cookies`, `/refund`, and the
email footer is DaysGraphic LLC's **registered agent address**
(Entity Protect Registered Agent Services LLC, sold via Tailor Brands).

Entity Protect **only forwards official documents** (service of process,
government correspondence) — they don't forward general business mail.
That's technically a CAN-SPAM gap: the FTC strictly wants a postal
address where the sender can be reached for ANY mail, not just legal
documents. In practice, enforcement on small SaaS is near zero and no
real customer mails paper letters anyway.

**Defer the proper fix** (a virtual mailbox like Anytime Mailbox /
Stable / iPostal1 / Earth Class Mail, $10-15/mo) until either:
- a real customer/agency actually mails the address, OR
- email volume scales past ~500/day (regulator-bait territory)

When the time comes to swap to a virtual-mailbox address: update the
four legal pages (`/privacy` `/terms` `/cookies` `/refund`) + the
email layout (`api/resources/views/mail/layouts/bookready.blade.php`)
+ this doc. Registered agent stays the same — the two roles are
deliberately separate.
